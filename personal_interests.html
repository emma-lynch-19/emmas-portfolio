My interests outside of computing

Gardening

When it's not term time, I like to grow herbs and vegetables in the garden. I find it very rewarding to raise something from a tiny seed into a fully grown plant. I also like cooking, and love to include home-grown herbs and vegetables in my dishes. When I'm at university, I like to volunteer in the local community garden.

Piano & Classical Music

I play the piano. A friend taught me in sixth form, and I've been keeping it up since. I'm currently working towards my grade 3. I also enjoy going to listen to classical music and piano performances with my friend. I always think it's amazing how much variety and emotion you can get from just one intrusment.

Woodworking

I like the contrast between doing something intellectually challenging and something practical, like woodworking. I built a set of flower boxes for our local scout hut, and planted flowers that match the group's colours. My sister painted the group name and motto on the front too.

Hiking

I love the challenge of hiking hills and fells. Each year I go to the lake district with a group of friends, and we enjoy the amazing walks available there. We particularly like Catbells, Latrigg, Walla Crag and walks around Dertwentwater.
